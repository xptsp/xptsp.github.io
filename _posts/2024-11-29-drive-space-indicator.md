Drive Space Indicator helps you stay on top of available disk space by doing the following: it changes the icons you see in Computer (or My Computer) and puts a progress bar under each icon; the progress bar displays how full your drives are. On top of that, Drive Space Indicator can put icons in the system tray, icons that present the same progress bar.
